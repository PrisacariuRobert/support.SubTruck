Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

At SubTruck, we are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and store your information when you use our app and services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Names, email addresses, and team memberships for account creation and management.
  • Subscription Details: Data about your subscriptions, including costs, renewal dates, and categories.
  • Usage Data: Analytics to improve our services, such as app performance and user behavior.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide you with the best possible experience, including:

  • Managing and displaying your subscriptions.
  • Sending reminders for upcoming renewals.
  • Providing customer support and troubleshooting issues.

Your Privacy Choices

You can manage your data and preferences by adjusting your settings within the app. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us at privacy@subtruck.com.
